4. End of Unit 4 and Self-assessment

🎉 Unit 4 Complete: Learn from Every Challenge

🎉 Congratulations!

You’ve successfully completed Unit 4 – Problem solving at work!

🏆 Your Achievements

In this unit, you’ve developed key communication skills to manage problems, explain what went wrong, and offer professional solutions. You’ve:

  • Described common workplace problems and their consequences
  • Used conditional structures to explain, reflect and propose actions
  • Expanded your vocabulary to talk about support, incidents and communication breakdowns
  • Written a formal email in response to a customer complaint
  • Recorded a spoken message to report an incident and suggest improvements

🧠 Why Reflection Matters

Taking time to reflect helps you:

  • Recognise what you can already do — and what you want to improve
  • Strengthen your Competence to Learn to Learn
  • Support your Competence for Lifelong Learning
  • Build your confidence in complex and stressful workplace situations

Be honest. Celebrate your progress. Set your next communication goal.

📝 Personal Self-Assessment

🔤 Language & Communication

  • Can you describe a problem clearly and professionally?
  • Can you offer support or apologise using appropriate tone and expressions?
  • Do you feel more confident dealing with difficult situations in English?

📘 Grammar & Vocabulary

  • Can you use conditional structures to talk about real or imaginary scenarios?
  • Are you using vocabulary related to problems and solutions more fluently?
  • Can you explain causes and consequences in a structured way?

💡 Learning Process

  • Did the planning sheets help you organise your ideas before speaking or writing?
  • Did you apply feedback from previous tasks to improve your performance?
  • Have you started to reflect more actively on how you learn?

🚀 Looking Ahead

  • What communication skill helped you the most in this unit?
  • What do you want to keep improving in the next unit?
  • How can you continue building your confidence and flexibility at work?

👏 Keep Moving Forward

Every challenge is an opportunity. Well done on everything you’ve achieved. Take a moment to reflect, feel proud — and get ready for Unit 5. You’re growing into a stronger, more resourceful communicator every day.
