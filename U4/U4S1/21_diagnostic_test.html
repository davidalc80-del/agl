2.1. Diagnostic test

Get Ready to Check Your Grammar! 🧠

Before we explore how to use conditional sentences at work, let’s take a moment to see what you already know. This diagnostic test will help you review the three most common conditional forms in English — and understand when and why we use them:

  • Zero Conditional – for general truths and workplace rules
  • First Conditional – for real and possible future situations
  • Second Conditional – for imaginary or hypothetical ideas and polite advice

What’s the goal? 🏁

To help you identify which structures you already use well — and which ones you might want to review before moving on.

How? ✅

Take the test and check your answers at the end using the Answer Key.

Scoring: 📊

  • 80% or more (16–20 points): Great! You’re ready for the next step.
  • Less than 80% (below 16 points): No worries — review the grammar section and try again with confidence.

This activity supports your Competence to Learn to Learn and your Competence for Lifelong Learning — both essential for success in today’s workplace.

Diagnostic Test – Conditionals 🧪

Part 1: Multiple Choice 🔹

Choose the best answer to complete the sentence.

💡 Use Zero Conditional for general facts, First Conditional for real future possibilities, and Second Conditional for hypothetical situations.

  • 1. If you don’t water the plant, it __________.

  • 2. If I were the manager, I __________ more training.

  • 3. If she works overtime, she __________ more tired.

  • 4. If you arrive late again, the supervisor __________.

  • 5. If the lights go out, the emergency system __________ on.

  • 6. If the team shared responsibilities, workers __________ less stressed.

  • 7. If they don’t take breaks, their productivity __________.

  • 8. If I had more time, I __________ a second internship.

Part 2: Complete the Sentence 🔹

Write the correct verb form in the blank.

💡 Use Zero Conditional for general facts, First Conditional for real future possibilities, and Second Conditional for hypothetical situations.

  • 10. If we finish early, we __________ (help) the logistics team.

  • 11. If he __________ (be) more organised, he would meet deadlines.

  • 12. If employees __________ (feel) safe, they work better.

  • 13. If the machine broke, we __________ (call) technical support.
