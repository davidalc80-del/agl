Unit 4: Problem solving at work - Session 1

Session 1 – Workplace Problems and Solutions

Introduction

Starting a new project, solving an issue, or reporting a mistake are all part of daily work life. In this session, you’ll learn how to discuss common workplace problems and how professionals address them.

Learning Objectives

Through reading, vocabulary, and grammar activities, you’ll learn to:

  • Understand and describe typical workplace challenges.
  • Identify causes and consequences of problems.
  • Use appropriate words to describe issues and solutions.
  • Practice conditional sentences to discuss consequences and make suggestions.

Skills You'll Develop

You’ll practice reading skills and review useful verbs and expressions to explain, report, and solve real-world professional situations.

  • Reading comprehension of workplace scenarios.
  • Using precise vocabulary to articulate problems and solutions.
  • Applying conditional sentences in professional communication.

Why This Matters

This session will equip you to handle problems and communicate solutions with clarity and confidence.

Let’s get started and tackle workplace challenges like a pro!
