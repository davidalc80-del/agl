3. Vocabulary

Vocabulary and Practice: Workplace Problems and Solutions 🛠️

Words matter — especially at work. When something goes wrong, it’s important to describe the situation clearly and professionally. Is it a delay? A mistake? A technical issue? And what happens next — do you investigate, report, replace, or compensate?

In this section, you’ll practise using vocabulary to describe real workplace situations and how to respond to them. You’ll see how different problems can affect your team, clients, or deadlines — and what actions professionals take to solve them.

You’ll work with two key vocabulary areas:

  • Problems and Causes – to describe what’s going wrong
  • Solutions and Actions – to express how to deal with those problems

Use these words in the exercises to prepare for writing reports, making suggestions, and communicating more effectively at work. Ready? Let’s get started.
