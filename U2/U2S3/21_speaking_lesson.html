2.1. Speaking Lesson

Speaking Lesson: Fostering Understanding in the Workplace

Lesson Objectives

This lesson equips you with skills to navigate workplace interactions effectively. You will learn to:

  • Discuss workplace challenges with clarity.
  • Analyze misunderstandings, including cultural influences.
  • Offer polite, constructive advice to newcomers.
  • Structure your speech for fluency and empathy.

These skills prepare you for:

  • Practice Task: Narrating a Workplace Misunderstanding
  • Final Task: Advising a New Colleague from Poland

1. Understanding Workplace Misunderstandings

Misunderstandings often arise from:

  • Differing expectations (e.g., approaches to deadlines or collaboration).
  • Missing context (e.g., a comment seems abrupt without background).
  • Cultural norms (e.g., directness in feedback or formality levels).

4. Structuring Your Speech

Use this clear framework for both tasks:

  • Introduce: Set the scene briefly.
  • Explain: Describe the issue or cultural difference.
  • Reflect or Advise: Share lessons or suggestions.
  • Conclude: End with an optimistic note.

This structure keeps your thoughts organized and engaging.
