2.1. Writing Lesson

Writing Lesson: Crafting Professional and Precise Texts

Why This Matters

Effective professional writing conveys confidence, respect, and clarity. Whether describing your strengths or offering advice, the way you express ideas can significantly impact your message. This lesson focuses on:

  • Using boosters to project confidence.
  • Applying hedges for politeness and flexibility.
  • Avoiding overly direct or informal language to maintain professionalism.

1. Boosters: Conveying Confidence

Boosters are words or phrases that strengthen your statements, ideal for showcasing skills or making firm recommendations.

Hedges help you offer suggestions or opinions without sounding overly assertive, making your writing more approachable and professional.

Start with a simple sentence and enhance it using boosters or hedges for a more professional tone.

Key Takeaway

Adapt your tone to your audience. To maintain professionalism:

  • Use boosters to assert confidence in your skills or recommendations.
  • Apply hedges to soften suggestions and maintain courtesy.
  • Opt for precise, formal vocabulary over vague or casual expressions.
