3. Vocabulary

📚 Vocabulary – Workplace Values and Behaviours

Introduction

In every job, how we behave with others matters. Companies don’t just need people who know how to do their tasks — they also need professionals who act with respect, integrity, and fairness. In this section, you’ll learn key vocabulary to talk about positive values that help create a good work environment, and problematic behaviours that can damage trust and teamwork.

You will explore words that describe:

  • What companies should promote (like equality or accountability)
  • What professionals mustn’t do (like gossip or show favouritism)

This vocabulary will help you describe ethical situations, take part in discussions, and explain your point of view clearly and professionally. You’ll also connect the new words with your own experience in real or future workplaces.

💡 Tip: Think about how these words can help you communicate in your future job.
