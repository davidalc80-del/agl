Interactive Vocabulary Practice – Workplace Values and Behaviours

🧠 Activity 1 – Match and Complete

Part A – Match the Definitions

Instructions: Match each value or behavior to its definition by selecting from the dropdown. Click "Check Answers" for feedback.

  • 1. Fairness
  • 2. Gossip
  • 3. Accountability
  • 4. Harassment
  • 5. Transparency

Part B – Complete the Sentences

Instructions: Type the correct word from the box (respect, favoritism, integrity, discrimination, trust) to complete each sentence. Click "Check Answers" for feedback.

  • 6. In an ethical team, all members must show _____ in their work.
  • 7. The HR department must avoid any kind of _____ during hiring.
  • 8. A manager who gives extra benefits to a friend is showing _____.
  • 9. Employees need to act with _____ even when no one is watching.
  • 10. Team leaders have to build _____ by being consistent and honest.

💡 Tip: For Part A, match the term to its definition carefully. For Part B, choose a term from the box that fits the ethical context.

🧠 Activity 2 – Classify and Reflect

Instructions: Read each workplace situation and type the matching vocabulary term (from the values or behaviors list) in the text box. The type (Value or Behavior) is provided. Click "Check Answers" for feedback.

  • 1. A team leader shares decisions clearly with the group. (Value)
  • 2. An employee talks negatively about a coworker’s personal life. (Behavior)
  • 3. A company gives equal chances to all candidates. (Value)
  • 4. A manager ignores complaints from a junior employee. (Behavior)
  • 5. A supervisor gives the best shifts only to his friends. (Behavior)

💡 Tip: Use the type (Value or Behavior) to guide your choice of term—values are positive, behaviors are problematic.
