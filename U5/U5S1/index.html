Unit 5: Professional values and ethics - Session 1

🎯 Session 1: Understanding Workplace Values

Welcome to Session 1

What makes a workplace truly ethical? In this session, you’ll discover the values that create respectful and fair environments. Through a reading on fostering ethical behavior, you’ll explore how companies go beyond rules to promote integrity with everyday actions. You’ll also learn vocabulary to describe core values like respect, integrity, and accountability, and identify problematic behaviors, such as gossip or bias.

Grammatically, you’ll review and practice modal verbs like must, should, ought to, and mustn’t to express workplace rules, expectations, and advice.

By the end of this session, you’ll be able to:

  • Discuss workplace values and behaviors with confidence.
  • Use modal verbs to give advice and outline rules.
  • Reflect on how ethics apply in real professional scenarios.

This session lays the groundwork for the unit, equipping you with the language and insights to navigate workplace dilemmas, offer recommendations, and communicate ethically in your future career.

🚀 Let’s get started!
