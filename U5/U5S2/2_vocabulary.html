2. Vocabulary

Vocabulary – Describing Ethical Challenges at Work

Introduction

In this session, you will learn how to talk about real workplace challenges where ethics, fairness and respect are at risk. When someone is asked to do extra work without recognition, or when people are treated differently based on gender or role, we need precise words to describe what is happening — and to talk about what should be done.

You will review and practise:

  • Key terms like glass ceiling, bias, burnout, and fairness
  • Phrasal verbs like speak up, stand up for and own up to
  • Useful expressions with modal verbs to give advice and suggest action

This vocabulary will help you describe situations, express your opinion, and respond professionally when something is not right at work.

💡 Tip: Think about how these words can help you communicate professionally in challenging workplace situations.
