Unit 5: Professional values and ethics - Session 2

Session 2 – Ethical Challenges at Work

Introduction

In any workplace, people can face difficult situations: unfair treatment, unequal opportunities, or extra work that goes unnoticed. These challenges often affect motivation, trust and team dynamics. In this session, you will listen to real examples of ethical problems, and you’ll learn how to describe them clearly and professionally.

You will explore:

  • How to recognise and talk about unethical behaviours at work
  • What values like fairness, inclusion or accountability look like in practice
  • How to use phrasal verbs and modal expressions to respond to ethical issues

You’ll also learn key vocabulary from the listening and practise using expressions like You should speak up or They mustn’t ignore this issue. This will help you respond appropriately to difficult situations and express your views in a respectful and confident way.

Tip: Focus on how the vocabulary and expressions can help you communicate professionally in ethical discussions.
