1. Key Equipment and Systems in Food Processing

What equipment ensures food safety and efficiency? These terms describe the systems and tools used in food processing, from conveyors to cleaning systems, aiding in production line explanations or staff training.

Term Meaning Example Sentence
conveyor belt Moving belt that transports products. The conveyor belt moves the jars to the filling station.
dosing system System that distributes precise product quantities. We use a dosing system to fill each container with 100g.
metal detector Device that detects metal in food products. All packaged products go through the metal detector.
pasteurisation tunnel Equipment for heat treatment of packaged products. The juices go through the pasteurisation tunnel after filling.
CIP system (Clean-in-Place) Automatic cleaning system for production lines. The CIP system is activated at the end of each shift.
Use pasteurisation tunnel and CIP system in technical reports or audits. Conveyor belt is useful for casual explanations or training.

3. Describing Changes and Improvements

How do you announce a new system or improved process? These expressions clarify updates and improvements in food processing, ensuring effective communication in reports, audits, or briefings.

Expression Example Sentence
introduce a new labelling system We introduced a digital labelling system with traceability codes.

Use optimise the filling process and improve shelf life in proposals or quality reports. Introduce a new labelling system is conversational for team updates.
