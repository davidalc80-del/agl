1. Types of Risks and Safety Concepts

What risks threaten workplace safety? These terms describe common hazards and safety measures, aiding in risk explanations or worker training.

Term Meaning Example Sentence
occupational hazard Potential source of harm in the workplace. Noise is a common occupational hazard in manufacturing.
personal protective equipment (PPE) Safety equipment worn by workers. Wearing PPE is mandatory in the laboratory.
safety signage Signs indicating risks, obligations, or prohibitions. Check that all safety signage is visible and up to date.
ergonomic risk Risk caused by poor body posture or repetitive tasks. Office staff face ergonomic risks from prolonged sitting.
fire prevention system Equipment and plans to prevent or control fire. The fire prevention system includes alarms and extinguishers.
Use occupational hazard and fire prevention system in safety audits or reports. PPE is useful for casual instructions or training.

2. Typical Actions and Responsibilities

What tasks ensure a safe workplace? These actions and expressions describe key responsibilities, like risk assessments or incident reporting, essential for procedure documentation or instructions.

Action/Expression Use in Context
carry out a risk assessment We carried out a full risk assessment before the project began.
evaluate exposure to hazards We evaluate noise exposure using a dosimeter.
design an evacuation plan The evacuation plan is displayed in every corridor.
implement corrective measures The company implemented corrective measures after the inspection.
report a workplace incident All incidents must be reported within 24 hours.
Use carry out a risk assessment and implement corrective measures in formal reports or compliance documents. Report a workplace incident is great for quick instructions or training.

3. Describing Preventive Measures and Documentation

How do you promote safety or document compliance? These expressions clarify preventive measures and documentation, ensuring effective communication in audits, training, or guidelines.

Expression Example Sentence
comply with safety regulations All workers must comply with the safety regulations.
train employees in emergency procedures We train new employees in emergency procedures on day one.
review safety documentation Please review the safety file before the audit.
inspect safety equipment Inspect the fire extinguishers once a month.
reduce the risk of accidents Good housekeeping reduces the risk of accidents.
Use comply with safety regulations and review safety documentation in audit reports or legal compliance. Train employees in emergency procedures is conversational for team briefings.
