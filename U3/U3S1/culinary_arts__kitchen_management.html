1. Key Equipment and Systems in a Professional Kitchen

What tools keep a kitchen running smoothly? These terms describe the equipment and systems used in professional kitchens, from chillers to workstations, aiding in setup explanations or staff training.

Term Meaning Example Sentence
blast chiller Machine to reduce temperature rapidly. We use the blast chiller for food safety after cooking.

2. Actions and Procedures in Kitchen Management

What tasks drive an efficient kitchen? These actions and expressions describe key procedures, like organizing production or checking inventory, essential for instructions or workflow documentation.

Action/Expression Use in Context

Use organise the production plan and check the inventory in formal reports or shift plans. Portion the dishes is ideal for quick staff instructions.

3. Describing Changes and Improvements

How do you announce a new technique or safety rule? These expressions clarify changes and improvements in the kitchen, ensuring effective communication in briefings, reports, or menus.

Expression Example Sentence

Use implement HACCP procedures and optimise the prep sequence in safety audits or efficiency reports. Introduce a new plating technique is conversational for team updates.
